About Our Platform

What is Re-Earth?

Re-Earth is a waste bank platform that empowers individuals and communities to take action against plastic pollution. By providing an easy-to-use system for sorting and depositing household waste, Re-Earth encourages sustainable habits and rewards users for their efforts. Our mission is to create a cleaner and greener environment by transforming daily waste into valuable resources, fostering a sense of community, and increasing environmental responsibility.

Frequently Asked Questions

All you need to know about how our waste bank works, its benefits, and how you can participate.

Re-Earth Waste Bank is a platform that allows individuals to deposit their household waste and earn rewards. We provide collection, weighing, and waste management services to help reduce plastic waste and increase environmental awareness.
To join the Re-Earth Waste Bank, you can contact us through our "Contact Us" page
Before depositing, please separate your trash into categories such as plastics, paper, glass, and metals. Ensure they are clean, dry, and flattened or compressed if possible to make the weighing process easier.
You can drop off your sorted trash at the collection point nearest to you. We have multiple drop-off locations across the city.
The rates per kilogram vary depending on the type and quality of the waste (e.g., PET bottles, cardboard, or aluminum cans). You can view our updated daily waste price list directly on Our Price List.
