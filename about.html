Who We Are

Restoring Our Planet,
One Step at a Time.

We are a global community dedicated to protecting natural ecosystems, fighting climate change, and building a sustainable future.

Our Journey

Re-Earth began in 2015 as a small grassroots initiative formed by a group of passionate environmentalists who refused to stand by as our local forests faced unprecedented deforestation. What started as weekend tree-planting campaigns and community clean-ups quickly resonated with thousands of people who shared a common dream: a thriving, green earth.

Over the past decade, we have grown into a global non-profit organization. We have successfully planted over 5 million trees across three continents, cleaned 10,000 tons of plastic from our oceans, and educated countless communities on sustainable living. Our journey is a testament to the power of collective action.

Our Vision

"A world where humanity and nature thrive in perfect harmony."

Our Mission

Ecosystem Restoration

Actively restore degraded forests, wetlands, and oceans through large-scale, science-backed regeneration projects worldwide.

Community Empowerment

Equip local and indigenous communities with the resources and knowledge to become the primary guardians of their natural habitats.

Policy Advocacy

Work alongside governments and corporations to advocate for structural changes, strict environmental protections, and sustainable policies.

Global Education

Inspire and educate the next generation of environmental leaders through accessible school programs and public awareness campaigns.
